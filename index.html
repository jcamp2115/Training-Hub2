Training Progress Checklist

Track your learning progress with this comprehensive checklist. Both you and your supervisor should sign off on completed items.

Week 1-2: Orientation & Basics

Complete HIPAA training and privacy protocols
Learn EMR system navigation and basic entry
Practice patient check-in procedures
Understand appointment scheduling system

Week 3-4: Clinical Tasks

Master chart preparation workflow
Learn insurance verification process
Practice telehealth platform operation (Virtual MAs)
Complete medication reconciliation training

Week 5-6: Advanced Skills

Handle complex patient communications
Manage referral coordination
Process lab results and follow-ups
Complete quality assurance protocols
